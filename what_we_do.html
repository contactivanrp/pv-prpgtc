Pasir Ris - Punggol Town Council

Our Story

From our humble beginnings as Eunos Town Council, navigating through the change of electoral boundaries at the end of 2001, to our present-day formation as Pasir Ris - Punggol Town Council, our journey mirrors the dynamic evolution of our vibrant communities. This transition marked a significant milestone in our commitment to community service and collaboration, we have remained steadfast in our commitment to serve alongside our residents through change.

Spanning the picturesque landscapes of Pasir Ris and Punggol, we manage HDB estates in the Pasir Ris - Punggol GRC and Punggol West SMC, hosting over 80,000 households, with new developments continually enriching our community. As the nucleus of the north-eastern shoreline, we take pride in fostering a sense of belonging among our diverse residents.

To ensure that our town remains the preferred choice for residents, we are represented by five Members of Parliament for Pasir Ris - Punggol GRC and one Member of Parliament for Punggol West SMC. Currently, Mr Teo Chee Hean, Senior Minister and Coordinating Minister for National Security, sits on our Council as Advisor. Led by our Chairman, Mr Sharael Taha; with our Vice Chairmen, Dr Janil Puthucheary; Senior Minister of State, Ministry of Health Government Whip, Ministry of Communications and Information, and Ms Sun Xueling; Minister of State, Ministry of Home Affairs & Ministry of Social and Family Development, Mr Desmond Tan; Minister of State, Prime Minister Office, Elected Members, Ms Yeo Wan Ling.

Our team, guided by a dedicated Council led by Chairman Mr. Sharael Taha and supported by esteemed Members of Parliament ensures that our community thrives. Together, we strive to create a collaborative environment where every voice matters.

With a team of over 70 professionals, we uphold the highest standards of estate maintenance, ensuring that every corner of our town reflects the pride and care of its residents. But we don't stop there – we're committed to continuous improvement, guided by your valuable feedback.

As we journey forward, we invite you to join us in shaping the future of our community. Together, we'll continue to build a home where every resident's voice is heard, and every contribution makes a difference. Welcome to Pasir Ris Punggol, where collaboration and community spirit thrive!

Our Mission

We will work with our residents and all stakeholders to:
  • listen to our residents’ concerns, and uphold high standards of cleanliness and maintenance for our towns with consistency
  • care for our residents’ needs through further improvements to the living conditions of our towns
  • develop a sustainable environment with sound financial prudency and governance
  • nurture inclusive communities with graciousness and respect
