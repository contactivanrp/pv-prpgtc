Executive Committee (EXCO)

The Council’s Exco comprises the Advisor, Chairman, Vice Chairmen, Elected Members, Secretary and the chairmen of all Standing Committees. The Exco acts for and on behalf of Council to advise, establish, implement, oversee and review the Town Council’s corporate plan.
